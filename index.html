About Me About

I’m an innovator, a systems thinker, and a believer in creating things which meaningfully touch and improve people’s lives. Currently, I'm building cutting-edge hardware products with Microsoft's Applied Sciences incubation team.

I studied electrical engineering at the University of Colorado, and am finishing a master's in human-centered design & engineering at the University of Washington. I create at the intersection of these disciplines, leading the development of highly impactful products and experiences in response to uniquely unraveled human challenges.

In my spare time, you'll find me snapping photos in new places, grooving behind a pair of turntables, kayaking the Puget Sound, teaching students about robotics, and doting on my wonderful cat, Rocco. I'm always cooking away on a side project or two. Want to make something cool together? Drop me a line.
