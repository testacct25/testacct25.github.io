About Me About

I’m an innovator, a systems thinker, and a believer in creating things which meaningfully touch and improve people’s lives.

Currently, I'm building cutting-edge hardware products within Microsoft's Applied Sciences incubation team. I studied electrical engineering at the University of Colorado, and am wrapping up a master's in human-centered design & engineering at the University of Washington.

My favorite moments involve bringing these skillsets together to solve complex human problems.

In my spare time, you'll find me snapping photos, making mostly pleasant sounds with turntables, kayaking, teaching students about robotics, exploring new places, and almost always cooking away on a side project.
